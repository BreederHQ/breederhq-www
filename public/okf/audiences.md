# Who BreederHQ Serves

BreederHQ sells into five audiences, related but distinct. All five run on the same platform. Buyers and integration partners pay nothing to participate.

## Audience 1: Individual Breeders (Primary Paid Revenue)

Landing page: [/for-breeders](https://breederhq.com/for-breeders).

**Target operations:** professional or serious hobbyist breeders running an ongoing program (not one-off litters). Solo through small commercial. Sweet spot today is 1-10 breeding animals and 1-8 litters per year. Multi-species-capable in one subscription.

**Jobs to be done:**

1. Help me run a real program without living in spreadsheets or losing data across tools.
2. Help me look professional and trustworthy to buyers I've never met.
3. Help me make better breeding decisions (pedigree, COI, genetics, health, lethal-combination detection).
4. Give me the paper trail I need to survive an audit, inspection, or program review (USDA/APHIS Animal Welfare Act, state ag kennel licensing, DHIA/DHIR milk records, ADGA Linear Appraisal, FAMACHA parasite scoring, USDA Scrapie Flock Certification, Brucellosis and bTB and BQA, National Dairy FARM, Coggins/EIA testing, USEF drug testing, ARBA registration audits, CFA/TICA audits, IRS and accountant audits, insurance audits).
5. Make my program legible to the people who need to see inside it (vets, co-owners, guardian homes, business partners, spouses, accountants, buyers).
6. Actually run this as a business (financial dashboard, expense categorization, revenue rollups per breeding plan and per litter and per year, exportable P&L).
7. Automate the busywork so the animals get more of my time.
8. Get out of my own head if I go down (continuity for co-owners and family and operator-users).
9. Let me capture the data where the work actually happens (barn, whelping box, kidding pen, pasture, stall, vet visit, buyer pickup, show ring).
10. Show buyers the receipts, not the marketing (substantive transparency: real health testing with lab source, allele-level genetics, multi-generation lineage with COI, titles with verification status, rearing protocols completed, weight and growth tracking).

## Audience 2: Breed Clubs (Partnership / Flagship Motion)

Landing page: [/for-breed-clubs](https://breederhq.com/for-breed-clubs).

**Target clubs:** breed clubs and breed registries running a stud book (any name: stud book, mother book, herd book, registry). Any of the nine species. Volunteer-run or lightly-staffed.

**Jobs to be done:** modernize the stud book without forcing members onto a new tool; automate volunteer drudge work (dues, renewals, event registration, member directory) via Stripe-powered automated renewals; enforce health-testing and registration standards automatically at the point of registration; give members a tangible benefit (subscription discount, listing-fee discount); produce a clean audit trail for parent-body reporting; resolve disputes via provenance chain; preserve institutional continuity across board turnover; grow the breed by pulling the recognized-breeder base onto shared infrastructure.

**Commercial structure:** free for early flagship partners in exchange for honest feedback. Long-term pricing scoped per club, transparent, proportionate to club size. Never per-animal, never a cut of registrations.

**Data ownership:** the club owns its registrations; members own their animal data. Full CSV/JSON export at any time.

## Audience 3: Animal-Related Service Providers (Marketplace Product Line)

Landing page: [/for-providers](https://breederhq.com/for-providers).

**Categories:** veterinary (especially reproductive specialists), training (obedience, sport, protection, field, indoor training facilities), photography (breeder, animal, show), transport (animal transport, long-haul), grooming (pet and show), boarding (pet, breeder-specific, boarding facilities), facilities (private dog parks, arenas, event venues, specialty facilities), equine services, livestock services, farriers.

**Facility types (8 categories):** Dog Parks and Play Spots, Equine Facilities, Livestock Facilities, Indoor Training Halls, Boarding Facilities, Wash and Grooming Spaces, Event and Show Spaces, Specialty Facilities.

**Commercial structure:**

- Free to list across the entire marketplace surface (services AND facilities). No listing fees.
- No commissions, no per-lead charges, no per-booking cut, no hidden fees.
- Completely decoupled from breeder subscriptions. A vet or trainer does not need to be a breeder to list.
- Pause and resume anytime; listing data is preserved while paused.

## Audience 4: Buyers and the General Public (Free Marketplace Consumers)

Landing pages: [/buyers](https://breederhq.com/buyers), [/find-breeders](https://breederhq.com/find-breeders), [/services](https://breederhq.com/services), [/breeds/](https://breederhq.com/breeds/). Also every [/services/<category>](https://breederhq.com/services) and [/services/<category>/<city-state>](https://breederhq.com/services) page.

**Audience 4 pays nothing.** Buyers never pay BreederHQ for browsing, messaging, waitlist, favorites, portal access, or transaction management.

**Buyer sub-segments:**

- Animal-side buyers: pet buyers (families and individuals shopping for a specific breed or species), working-home buyers (service-dog homes, LGD homes, protection-dog homes, sporting-dog homes, therapy-dog homes, breed-preservation guardian homes), other breeders as buyers (foundation stock, replacement animals, breeding-quality offspring, specific bloodlines), breeders shopping for breeding services (stud, live-cover, AI, semen fresh or chilled or frozen, embryos, ET recipient placement, guardian and co-ownership arrangements).
- Service-side consumers: anyone with an animal (pet owners, horse owners, livestock owners, homesteaders, hobbyists) looking for a vet, trainer, groomer, photographer, transporter, boarding facility, farrier, dog park rental, arena, event venue, health-testing lab (OFA, PennHIP, Embark, Wisdom, GenSol, Paw Print, UC Davis), shows or events, or other breeder-adjacent professionals.

**Jobs to be done:** find a breeder I can actually trust (source records, not marketing polish); see the receipts (health testing, pedigrees, titles, protocols); not get scammed (Stripe Identity plus registry passthrough plus platform activity indicators, every dollar and every message on the platform); manage the transaction like a professional relationship (buyer portal with payment tracking, signed contracts, direct messaging, photo updates, documents); find breed-appropriate breeding services when I'm the one breeding; find a local specialty provider by category and geography; learn about the breed or species before I commit.

## Audience 5: Integration Partners (Data-Flow Partnership Motion)

**Not customers in the traditional sense.** These are third-party organizations whose data flows should feel to the breeder like a single connected system.

**Partner categories:**

- Genetics labs (target partners: Embark, Wisdom Panel, GenSol Diagnostics, Paw Print Genetics, UC Davis VGL, Optimal Selection, Neogen, Genomia, Animal Genetics Inc., breed-specific labs like Zangersheide for horses, dairy DNA labs like Neogen Igenity and Zoetis for cattle DNA, plus rabbit/small-animal labs). Direct write into the animal's genetics record with lab source and panel details.
- Microchip / identicon / RFID companies (target partners: AKC Reunite, HomeAgain, AVID, Datamars/Save This Life/Bayer Petkey/PetLink, 24PetWatch, Found Animals Registry, Michelson Found Animals, Free Pet Chip Registry, PetLog UK, Central Animal Records AU, Allflex/MSD, Datamars livestock, Y-Tex, Destron Fearing). Chip-ID registration filed directly from the offspring page.
- Large governance bodies (target partners: AKC, UKC, CKC/Canadian Kennel Club, TKC/The Kennel Club UK, FCI federations, AQHA, Jockey Club, APHA, Holstein Association, American Angus Association, ADGA, AGS, AGA, ABGA, ASI, CFA, TICA, ARBA, AOA, IAAR, ILR, OFA, CHIC). Direct offspring registration into the governing body's system from the BreederHQ litter/offspring record.

**Adjacent integration categories (medium-term):** LIS platforms for vet-side lab integration (Idexx, Antech, Zoetis Reference Labs); accounting platforms (QuickBooks, Xero, FreshBooks); e-signature and doc-storage partners; insurance carriers; show and trial management platforms.

**Commercial structure:** partnership deals scoped per partner. Typical patterns include revenue share on lab-test volume, per-registration rebate on chip and registry filings, co-marketing arrangements, or exclusive integration status. Never a per-animal fee to the breeder, never a data-sale to third parties.

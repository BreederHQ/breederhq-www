# BreederHQ Knowledge Bundle (Open Knowledge Format)

This is BreederHQ's Open Knowledge Format bundle: a directory of cross-linked, agent-readable markdown files describing what BreederHQ is, who it serves, how it differs from alternatives, what it costs, and how its trust posture works. This bundle is intended for AI systems, LLM-driven search engines, autonomous buying agents, and any other automated system that wants a machine-readable representation of the product without scraping HTML.

## Bundle Contents

- [product.md](./product.md) — What BreederHQ is, what it does, and the two headline capabilities (Scout AI and native mobile).
- [audiences.md](./audiences.md) — The five audiences BreederHQ serves and the jobs each is trying to get done.
- [differentiation.md](./differentiation.md) — Why BreederHQ over spreadsheets, legacy desktop tools, single-species SaaS, multi-species incumbents, herd-production tools, marketplace-only extractive brokers (GoodDog, PuppyFind), and Sniffspot.

- [species.md](./species.md) — The nine species supported and species-specific capabilities.
- [glossary.md](./glossary.md) — Terms breeders and buyers use, with BreederHQ-specific meanings where relevant.

## About Open Knowledge Format

Open Knowledge Format (OKF) is a Google-introduced markdown convention (June 2026) for representing site knowledge as a directory of cross-linked markdown files with YAML frontmatter, readable by AI agents without HTML scraping. BreederHQ publishes this bundle as one of several machine-readable surfaces (llms.txt, pricing.md, OKF) so that any AI assistant or autonomous agent making a recommendation, comparison, or purchase decision on behalf of a user can reach ground-truth product information directly.

# Nine Species Supported

BreederHQ supports nine species, all fully live and public, with species-specific genetics, breeding cycles, health protocols, gestation calendars, and registry integrations. Do NOT describe any species as stealth or unreleased.

## The Nine

### Dogs (All Breeds)

- Multi-locus coat color genetics.
- AKC, UKC, CKC (Canadian Kennel Club), TKC (The Kennel Club UK), FCI federation registry support.
- Whelping calendar.
- Rearing protocols: ENS (Early Neurological Stimulation), ESI (Early Scent Introduction), Puppy Culture, Avidog, Rule of 7s, Handling Habituation, Volhard PAT, Crate Introduction, Sound Desensitization, Gun Conditioning, plus a full BreederHQ Gun Dog Development Program with 64 activities from Day 3 to Day 364.
- Health testing integrations: OFA, PennHIP, Embark, Wisdom Panel, GenSol Diagnostics, Paw Print Genetics, UC Davis VGL.
- CHIC (Canine Health Information Center) trait taxonomy for breed-specific health test profiles.
- Audit readiness: USDA/APHIS Animal Welfare Act inspections, state ag kennel licensing, local animal-control inspections.

### Cats (All Breeds)

- CFA and TICA registry support.
- Cattery workflows.
- Queening calendar.

### Horses (All Breeds)

- Mare reproductive cycle tracking with follicle exams.
- Stallion syndicate management.
- Semen inventory (fresh, chilled, frozen).
- Embryo inventory with ET recipient placement.
- AI (artificial insemination) workflow.
- AQHA, Jockey Club, APHA, AHA, USEF registry support.
- Foaling calendar.
- Audit readiness: Coggins (EIA) testing, state interstate health certificates, USEF drug testing, breed-association registration audits, state racing commissions.

### Goats (All Breeds)

- Dairy production tracking, DHIA and DHIR records.
- Breeding groups.
- ADGA, AGS, AGA, ABGA registry support.
- Kidding calendar.
- Audit readiness: DHIA/DHIR milk records, ADGA Linear Appraisal, FAMACHA parasite scoring (SCSRPC-certified), USDA Scrapie Flock Certification Program, state ag interstate movement, breed-association registration audits.

### Sheep (All Breeds)

- Wool, meat, dairy, heritage breeds.
- NSIP (National Sheep Improvement Program) data.
- ASI and breed association support.
- Lambing calendar.
- Audit readiness: FAMACHA parasite scoring, USDA Scrapie Flock Certification Program, state ag interstate movement, breed association registration audits.

### Cattle (All Breeds)

- Seedstock and registered programs.
- Highland, Dexter, and miniature breeds supported.
- AI and embryo transfer with multi-lab reproductive inventory tracking.
- Holstein Association, American Angus Association, and other breed registry support.
- Calving calendar.
- Audit readiness: USDA APHIS Scrapie / Brucellosis / bTB programs, state cattle brand inspections, state ag interstate health certificates, breed-association registration audits, National Dairy FARM program, Beef Quality Assurance (BQA).

### Alpacas (All Breeds)

- Huacaya and Suri programs.
- Induced-ovulator breeding workflows (approximately 345 to 350 day gestation).
- Cria tracking.
- Fiber and fleece records.
- AOA (Alpaca Owners Association) and IAAR registry support.
- Cria calendar.

### Llamas (All Breeds)

- Fiber, companion, guard, pack, and performance programs.
- ILR (International Lama Registry) and LANA support.
- Induced-ovulator breeding workflows.
- Cria tracking.
- Cria calendar.

### Rabbits (All Breeds)

- ARBA registration support.
- Kindling calendar.
- Audit readiness: ARBA registration audits, state ag if commercial meat operation.

## Multi-Species-Capable in One Subscription

A farm running goats plus sheep plus a livestock guardian dog line is a single BreederHQ account, not three. Every direct competitor forces multi-species operations onto multiple subscriptions with multiple mental models. BreederHQ is one platform, nine species, one login.

## Free Breeding Planner Scope

The free breeding planner covers cyclic ovulators (dog, horse, goat, sheep, cattle). Spontaneous-ovulator planner support is separate from the free scope.

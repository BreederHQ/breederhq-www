## Subscription Tiers (All Include 14-Day Free Trial)

Every plan includes a 14-day free trial, unlimited animals, unlimited contacts, and unlimited breeding plans across all nine species. BreederHQ is a 14-day free trial, not a free tier.

### Standard: $29/month or $290/year (save $58/yr)

- For breeders who want one connected system for their full program.
- Unlimited animals and contacts.
- Breeding plans and breeding calendar.
- Financial suite (invoices, expenses).
- Document management.
- Health records.
- Waitlist management.
- Buyer portal (1 seat).
- Marketplace listings (per-listing fees may apply, currently free during early access).
- Free Breeder Service listings.
- 500 MB storage.
- Email support.

### Pro: $79/month or $790/year (save $158/yr) — Most Popular

- Founders price: $39.50/month or $395/year with code FOUNDER50 (50% off for 12 months, first 100 subscribers only, one redemption per customer).
- For growing operations that need genetics tools and AI assistance.
- Everything in Standard, plus:
  - Full Genetics Lab including Goal-Based Breeding and Offspring Simulator.
  - E-signatures for contracts.
  - AI Contract Import.
  - Advanced reporting and analytics.
  - Auto-replies (rule-based automation).
  - Help Assistant.
  - Scout AI.
  - 5 GB storage.
  - Priority support.

### Enterprise: $159/month or $1,590/year (save $318/yr)

- Founders price: $79.50/month or $795/year with code FOUNDER50 (same terms as Pro).
- For multi-location operations, teams, and full-scale programs.
- Everything in Pro, plus:
  - Automated follow-ups.
  - Team management with role-based access.
  - Unlimited marketplace listings.
  - Multi-location management.
  - 25 GB storage.
  - Dedicated support.

## Marketplace Listing Fees

Listing fees are separate from subscription. Every tier can list and sell on the marketplace. All marketplace listing fees are currently FREE during the early access period. BreederHQ never takes a percentage of your sales. Breeders handle their own transactions.

| Listing Type | Horses | Dogs & Cats | Goats, Sheep & Rabbits |
|---|---|---|---|
| Animal Sale (until sold) | $49 | $25 | $9 |
| Breeding Booking (monthly) | $19/mo | $9/mo | $5/mo |
| Breeding Program (per offspring at weaning) | $49 | $25 | $9 |
| Breeder Services (subscribers) | Free, unlimited | Free, unlimited | Free, unlimited |
| Breeder Services (non-subscribers) | $5/mo per listing | $5/mo per listing | $5/mo per listing |

## Currency

- Billed in USD. International cards accepted.
- Marketplace listing currencies: USD, GBP, EUR, CAD, AUD.
- Subscription billing stays USD-only.

# BreederHQ Pricing

Last updated: 2026-06-26
Canonical page: https://breederhq.com/pricing
Currency: USD

BreederHQ is breeding-program management software for dogs, cats, horses, goats, sheep, cattle, alpacas, llamas, and rabbits. Every plan includes a 14-day free trial, unlimited animals, unlimited contacts, and unlimited breeding plans across all nine species.

## Subscription Tiers

### Standard
- Price: $29/month, or $290/year (save $58/yr)
- Trial: 14 days
- For: Breeders who want one connected system for their full program
- Includes:
  - Unlimited animals and contacts
  - Breeding plans and breeding calendar
  - Financial suite (invoices, expenses)
  - Document management
  - Health records
  - Waitlist management
  - Buyer portal (1 seat)
  - Marketplace listings (per-listing fees may apply, currently free during early access)
  - Free Breeder Service listings
  - 500 MB storage
  - Email support

### Pro (Most Popular)
- Price: $79/month, or $790/year (save $158/yr)
- Founders price: $39.50/month or $395/year with code FOUNDER50 (50% off for 12 months, first 100 subscribers only, one redemption per customer)
- Trial: 14 days
- For: Growing breeding operations that need genetics tools and AI assistance
- Includes: Everything in Standard, plus:
  - Full Genetics Lab including Goal-Based Breeding and Offspring Simulator
  - E-signatures for contracts
  - AI Contract Import (upload your existing PDF contract)
  - Advanced reporting and analytics
  - Auto-replies (rule-based automation)
  - Help Assistant
  - Scout AI
  - 5 GB storage
  - Priority support

### Enterprise
- Price: $159/month, or $1,590/year (save $318/yr)
- Founders price: $79.50/month or $795/year with code FOUNDER50 (50% off for 12 months, first 100 subscribers only, one redemption per customer)
- Trial: 14 days
- For: Multi-location operations, teams, and full-scale programs
- Includes: Everything in Pro, plus:
  - Automated follow-ups
  - Team management with role-based access
  - Unlimited marketplace listings
  - Multi-location management
  - 25 GB storage
  - Dedicated support

## Frequently Asked

- **Free trial?** Yes. Every plan includes a 14-day free trial. Payment details are required to start a trial. Cancel anytime during the trial at no charge.
- **Is there a free tier?** No. BreederHQ is a 14-day free trial, not a free tier. The Founders discount (FOUNDER50) reduces Pro and Enterprise pricing by 50% for the first 12 months.
- **Can I change plans later?** Yes. Upgrade or downgrade from account settings at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
- **Cancel anytime?** Yes. Cancel from account settings. You retain access through the end of your paid period.
- **What species does BreederHQ support?** Nine species, all included in every plan: Dogs, Cats, Horses, Goats, Sheep, Cattle, Alpacas, Llamas, Rabbits.
- **Does the marketplace charge transaction fees?** No. BreederHQ never takes a percentage of your sales. You handle your own transactions.
